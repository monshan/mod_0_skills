Class: Wait Staff

Attributes:
name (string)
age (integer)
priorExperience (boolean)
section (array, could have either integer or string type data to represent the area/tables of the restaurant that wait staff is in charge of for any particular work day)


Methods:
giveGreeting (returns a phrase with reference to the 'name' and/or 'age' variable)
clearTable (changes another boolean related to the tables assigned in the 'section' variable)
addExperience (can change priorExperience variable from true to false after passing a certain threshold of time with the restaurant)
reAssignTable (adds/takes away/changes the 'section' array)
